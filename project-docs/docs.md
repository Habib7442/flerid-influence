# InfluConnect Pro - Premium Influencer Marketing Platform Documentation

## 1. Project Overview

### Platform Concept
- **Name**: InfluConnect Pro
- **Type**: Premium B2B Influencer Marketing Platform
- **Model**: Curated/Managed Service (Fully Offline Registration)
- **Target**: Brands seeking quality influencer partnerships
- **USP**: Hand-picked, verified influencers with guaranteed authenticity

### Business Model
- You personally vet and add all brands and influencers
- No self-registration to maintain quality control
- Premium positioning through exclusivity
- Trust-first approach with personal relationship management

## 3. Color Scheme & Visual Identity

### Primary Color Palette

**Main Brand Colors:**
- **Primary Purple**: #667eea (Professional, premium, trustworthy)
- **Secondary Purple**: #764ba2 (Depth, sophistication)
- **Accent Pink**: #f093fb (Creative, energetic, modern)
- **Secondary Pink**: #f5576c (Bold, confident)

**Supporting Colors:**
- **Success Green**: #00d4aa (Growth, success, positive results)
- **Warning Orange**: #ff6b35 (Attention, energy, creativity)
- **Info Blue**: #4facfe (Trust, reliability, communication)
- **Neutral Gray**: #8892b0 (Balance, professionalism)

### Background Schemes
- **Dark Theme Primary**: #1a1a2e (Sophisticated, premium feel)
- **Card Backgrounds**: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.1) (Glass morphism effect)
- **Gradient Combinations**:
  - Hero: `linear-gradient(135deg, #667eea 0%, #764ba2 100%)`
  - CTA Buttons: `linear-gradient(135deg, #f093fb 0%, #f5576c 100%)`
  - Success Elements: `linear-gradient(135deg, #4facfe 0%, #00f2fe 100%)`

### Typography Guidelines
- **Headlines**: Bold, modern sans-serif (Montserrat/Poppins)
- **Body Text**: Clean, readable (Inter/Roboto)
- **Accent Text**: Semi-bold for emphasis
- **Color Usage**: White/light gray on dark backgrounds, dark text on light cards
